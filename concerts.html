    • The Farewell Effect: Ticket Prices Rise When Bands Announce a Final Tour

  • Scarcity drives demand, as they say. And when performers announce their final tour, the opportunity for fans to see their favorite artists in person immediately gets an expiration date.The result? A spike in ticket prices.Vivid Seats analyzed ticket price data around four performers who recently announced the their next tour will be their last: Elton John, Paul Simon, Slayer and Lynyrd Skynyrd.In each case, ticket prices immediately jumped up.
